Life of A Gamer Developer:

The work life of a game developer is generally comfortable, so a little bit of overtime in short
bursts is pretty easy to swallow. But when it goes on for too long–for weeks or even for months–that's
when it starts to cause problems with the developers' health and personal life.

Daily Activity of Gamer:

Game developers typically work during regular business hours—so 40 hours a week.
They may put in extra hours in the evenings and on weekends when a release deadline is nearing.
